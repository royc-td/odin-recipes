Mango Float

Home

Description

Mango Float is a no bake dessert with layers of cream, mango and graham crackers. This only uses 5 ingridients and perfect for the summer.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups heavy cream chilled overnight
  • 1/2 cup condensed chilld overnight
  • 4 ripe mangos chopped or sliced
  • 2 packs graham crackers from 1 box

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Peel the skin and slice the ripe mangos thinly or chop the mangos, then set aside

    Step 2

    Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form. Then gradually add a pinch of salt and condensed milk. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.

    Step 3

    Crush graham crackers until desired crumb or use the whole cracker without crushing.

    Step 4

    Start by layering the mango float in a 8x8 inch glass container or a large container. First lay the graham crackers. Then, whipped cream. Lastly, add sliced mango. Repeat one more time and for the last layer and whipped cream and sliced mango. Add some graham cracker crumbs at the top for decoration and texture.

    Step 5

    Cover the container and refrigerate overnight for soft texture. You can also freeze overnight for ice cream texture. Before eating, thaw for 30 minutes or less depending on what texture you like.
