Lumpia

Home

Description

This is a traditional Filipino recipe for lumpia, or fried spring rolls. They're made with lumpia wrappers and filled with a mixture of ground pork and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup minced carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green cabbage
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 30 lumpia wrappers
  • 2 cups vegetable oil

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over high heat. Add pork; cook and stir until crumbly and no longer pink - 5 to 7 minutes. Remove pork from the pan and set aside. Drain grease from the pan leaving a thin coating.

    Step 2

    Add onion and garlic to the pan; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes

    Step 3

    Stir in cooked pork, carrots, green onions and cabbage

    Step 4

    To assemble lumpa: Place 3 heaping tablespoons of filling diagonally near one corner of a lumpia wrapper, leaving a 1 1/2 inch space at both ends

    Step 5

    Fold the side along the length of the filling over the filling, tuck in both ends and roll neatly and tightly to close.

    Step 6

    Moisten the other side of the wrapper with water to seal edge. Transfer to a plate and cover with plastic wrap to retain moisture. Repeat this step to assemble remaining lumpia

    Step 7

    Heat 1/2 inch vegetable oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat for 5 minutes.

    Step 8

    Slide 3 to 4 lumpia into the hot oil, making sure the seams are facing down. Fry and turn occasionally until all sides are golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain. Repeat to fry remaining lumpia

    Step 9

    Serve immediately and enjoy!
